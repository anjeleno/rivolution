Patchbay

Refresh

Saved connections are automatically re-applied in the background (checked every 30s) whenever both ports exist again — no need to manually reconnect after a client restart. This talks directly to pw-link, not WirePlumber's own policy system, which does not apply to JACK-bridged ports (verified 2026-07-01, see spec 0007). A device that probes several instances while its I/O is being configured (e.g. Stereo Tool's ALSA/JACK driver) can leave a pile of unwanted auto-detected connections behind on a box where nothing has been saved yet — "Disconnect unsaved" clears all of them at once so you can connect and save just the ones actually wanted.

Program source

What feeds every broadcast stream. Pick "Stereo Tool" if this station processes locally with it — rivapi keeps Stereo Tool's own JACK target pointed at one of the streams automatically and fans its output out to the rest, since Stereo Tool itself can only ever be given one fixed target. Otherwise pick the JACK client whose output should feed the streams directly (e.g. Rivendell itself, on a station with no local processing). Pick the client, not a specific channel; its full stereo pair connects automatically to any newly added stream — a stream already connected is never repatched automatically, even if you change this; reconnect it by hand below instead. Saving here only updates the stored setting — it takes effect the next time streams are (re)deployed from Broadcast.
